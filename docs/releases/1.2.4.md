# DEGIRO Importer 1.2.4

## Highlights

- **Recoverable mapping review.** Stale remembered mappings now clearly show
  their status and can be replaced or removed without affecting other saved
  mappings.
- **Safe bulk-write recovery.** A host-level bulk rejection returns to a fresh
  mapping review instead of being presented as unrelated per-row failures.

## Compatibility

- Wealthfolio: 3.6.1
- Add-on SDK: 3.6.1
- DEGIRO account-statement CSV exports in Dutch or English header variants
